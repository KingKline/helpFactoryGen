Modes

Main

In the main mode the goal is to get the most amount of money before the season ends

The players with the highest scores will get real money payouts (paypal)

Endless

In endless you can build and grind as much as you want knowing that your factory will not reset at the end of the season

Playground

In Playground it is the creative mode for this server

you can easly get any item to play around or test which way you want to build your factory

How To Play

First do the command /start

This command will give you all the starter items and a plot to build your factory
